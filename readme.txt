== Description ==
**Affiliate Advantage**

Affiliate Advantage is an easy-to-use plugin that helps you sky-rocket your affiliate marketing business by providing a link management solution that allows you to shorten, cloak, and manage your links easily and efficiently.

Affiliate Advantage enables you to shorten and prettify your affiliate links using your own domain name as opposed to using some random link shortening service (like tiny.url or bit.ly).

Affiliate Advantage also comes with advanced link stats to help you understand how your affiliate links are performing and tons of other useful features to help you turbo-boost your affiliate marketing business.

Affiliate Advantage comes with one-click sharing functionality for all your created links, allowing you to easily share your affiliate links across multiple social networks.

== Installation ==
1. Upload ‘affiliate-advantage.zip’ to the ‘/wp-content/plugins/’ directory

2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress

3. Make sure you have changed your permalink Common Settings in Settings -> Permalinks away from “Default” to something else.
